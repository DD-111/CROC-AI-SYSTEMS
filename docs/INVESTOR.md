## Problem

Traditional site security stops at a bell or generic text. Every alert feels the same. Staff guess urgency and who should go. Camera footage sits unused. If nobody answers, the chain breaks. There is no audit trail.

**Who feels this pain:** operators of government buildings, malls, hospitals, campuses, commercial districts, public spaces, and residential communities — anywhere people must respond on site.

---

## Solution

**Croc Sentinel** — AI-powered monitoring, map, **3–30 s** phone alerts, camera context, iOS & Android apps.

**Croc Coordination** — urgency scoring (0–100), routing, escalation, human approval, full audit.

One AI stack: **detect → score → call → log → escalate** — with humans in control.
